# Ecosystem Overview

## IronRidge Contractors

IronRidge Contractors is a fictional mid-sized commercial contractor and field operations company based in Indianapolis, Indiana. The business spans General Contracting, Field Services, Special Projects, and Operations Support, with realistic internal challenges around coordination, reporting, and operational follow-through.

## The Four Demo Repositories

- `ops-visibility-demo` — executive reporting layer for project health, service backlog, closeout readiness, and risk
- `workflow-drag-reduction-demo` — internal support request workflow for intake, routing, approvals, and aging visibility
- `execution-infrastructure-demo` — lightweight execution tracker for commitments, blockers, ownership, and weekly review cadence
- `contractor-ops-system-demo` — field-aware operations tracker for issues, punch items, escalation, and closeout readiness

## How This Repository Fits

This repository represents the follow-through layer. It shows how IronRidge could create shared execution infrastructure so commitments do not disappear into meeting notes or goodwill after they are discussed.

## Shared Company Context

All four repos use the same company roster, project catalog, divisions, regions, and operating terminology so they read like connected parts of one internal systems environment.

## Portfolio Intent

This repo is meant to feel disciplined and credible: clear accountability logic, believable sample data, concise operating documentation, and a screenshot-ready review screen.
