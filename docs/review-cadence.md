# Review Cadence

## Weekly Rhythm

1. Department leads refresh action status before the weekly review.
2. Owners identify blockers and due-date risk before the meeting starts.
3. Weekly commitments are reviewed for completion, carry-forward, or escalation.
4. Overdue items are triaged by owner and department.
5. Material blockers are escalated to Rachel Holloway, David Sloan, or Caleb Dyer based on scope.

## Ownership Expectations

- every action item has one accountable owner
- blocked items require a stated blocker, not just a stale status
- overdue work should be discussed with a recovery date or escalation path
- commitments should stay lightweight and specific enough to review weekly
